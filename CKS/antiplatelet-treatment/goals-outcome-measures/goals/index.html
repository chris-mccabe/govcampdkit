Goals

To support primary healthcare professionals to:

  • Prescribe appropriate antiplatelet treatment for the primary and secondary prevention of cardiovascular disease.
  • Assess and reduce the risk of gastrointestinal bleeding associated with antiplatelet treatment.
  • Manage antiplatelet-associated dyspepsia.
